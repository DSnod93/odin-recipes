Pizza

Description

This is a pizza recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb. lean ground beef
  • 1/2 cup bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced provolone cheese
  • 1/2 cup sliced pepperoni
  • 1/2 cup sliced ham
  • 1/2 cup sliced onion
  • 1/2 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1/2 cup sliced red onion
  • 1/2 cup sliced spinach
  • 1/2 cup sliced tomato

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Mix ground beef, bread crumbs, Parmesan, mozzarella, provolone, pepperoni, ham, onion, mushrooms, red onion, spinach, and tomato together in a bowl.
  3. Pour mixture into a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes.
